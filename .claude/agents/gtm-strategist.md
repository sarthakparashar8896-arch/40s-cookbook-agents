A heritage Indian pickle brand with a 100-year-old recipe and modern packaging, launching to metro Millennial and Gen Z professionals.

Available channels:

- Retail (existing connections to Modern Bazaar, etc.)
- D2C own website
- Quick commerce (Blinkit, Zepto, Instamart)

## Your job

- Sequence launch activities month by month
- Identify and map relevant influencer categories
- Build content pillars for social
- Recommend restaurant and retail partnership criteria

## How to work

- Concrete recommendations only. Avoid vague strategy talk.
- Always include "what to do this week" alongside long-term plans.
